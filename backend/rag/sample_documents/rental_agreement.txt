RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is made on this day between Mr. Ramesh Iyer, residing at Flat 302, Sunrise Apartments, Andheri West, Mumbai ("Landlord"), and Ms. Kavita Menon, residing at present at 27 Church Street, Bengaluru ("Tenant").

1. PREMISES
The Landlord agrees to let out, and the Tenant agrees to take on rent, the residential premises situated at Flat 302, Sunrise Apartments, Andheri West, Mumbai, comprising two bedrooms, a hall, and a kitchen, together with the fittings and fixtures listed in Schedule A ("Premises").

2. TERM
The tenancy shall commence on the 1st of the month following execution of this Agreement and shall continue for a period of eleven (11) months, renewable thereafter by mutual written consent on such terms as the Parties may agree.

3. RENT
The Tenant shall pay a monthly rent of Rs. 35,000 (Rupees Thirty-Five Thousand only), payable in advance on or before the 5th day of each calendar month, by bank transfer to an account designated by the Landlord. A delay of more than seven (7) days shall attract a late fee of 2% of the monthly rent per week of delay.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant has paid an interest-free refundable security deposit of Rs. 1,05,000 (three months' rent) to the Landlord, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged. The deposit shall be refunded within thirty (30) days of vacating the Premises, subject to deductions for unpaid dues, damages beyond normal wear and tear, or unpaid utility bills.

5. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Tenant shall maintain the Premises in good and tenantable condition and shall be responsible for minor repairs up to Rs. 2,000 per instance. Major structural repairs shall be the responsibility of the Landlord. The Tenant shall not make any structural alterations without the Landlord's prior written consent.

6. UTILITIES
The Tenant shall be responsible for payment of electricity, water, and gas charges billed during the tenancy period. Society maintenance charges shall be borne by the Landlord unless otherwise agreed in writing.

7. USE OF PREMISES
The Premises shall be used solely for residential purposes by the Tenant and immediate family members. Subletting, in whole or in part, is prohibited without the prior written consent of the Landlord.

8. TERMINATION
Either Party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days' written notice to the other Party. In the event of non-payment of rent for two (2) consecutive months, the Landlord may terminate this Agreement with immediate effect after providing seven (7) days' notice to cure the default.

9. INSPECTION
The Landlord, or an authorized representative, may inspect the Premises with at least twenty-four (24) hours' prior notice to the Tenant, except in cases of emergency.

10. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the applicable rent control and tenancy laws of the State of Maharashtra, and any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the competent courts in Mumbai.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have set their hands on the date first written above, in the presence of the witnesses named below.
